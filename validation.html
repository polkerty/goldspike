Validation

 Forrest Brazeal (Last Modified January 5, 2012)

You will often run across a "captcha" while filling out a form on the Internet. These fuzzy images help keep computers from filling out the form millions of time with spam. We use an alternative to captchas. If you post something on our website, you may see something like this:

What's 1 + 1?

The answer, of course, is 2. We're just trying to ensure that we're getting feedback from people, and not robots.
