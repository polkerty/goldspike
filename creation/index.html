Evolution Will Never Adapt to This!

It's the white hats against the black hats in a bizarre battle to save the town of Worldview, Texas! Will the Lone Creationist make hamburger out of the sacred cows of evolution? Will the evolutionists detonate the Big Bang? Will little Dudley Sassafras ever hang up his gray hat and figure out what side he's actually on? You'll find your faith affirmed and your funny bone fractured in this wild Western adventure, featuring 11 original creation science songs kids will sing again and again!
